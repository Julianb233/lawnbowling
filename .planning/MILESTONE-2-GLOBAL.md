# Milestone 2: Global Expansion — World Domination

## Vision
Take Lawnbowling from USA-only to the #1 bowls platform worldwide. Target every major bowls market: UK, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, Canada, Asia.

## Revenue Model
- Per-member annual fee: $5-10/member/year
- Average club: 200 members = $1,000-$2,000/year
- Global TAM: 7,000+ clubs = $7-14M/year ARR

## Phases

### Phase 16: UK Market Entry (England + Scotland)
**Goal**: Enter the biggest untapped market — 2,900+ clubs, ZERO integrated tech platform
**Priority**: #1 (highest ROI, no competitor)
**Plans**:
- 16-01: Research all Bowls England clubs (2,000+), get names, contacts, board members
- 16-02: Research all Bowls Scotland clubs (900+), get names, contacts
- 16-03: Build /clubs/uk, /clubs/england, /clubs/scotland directory pages
- 16-04: Seed UK clubs into database with directors and key contacts
- 16-05: Localization — "Bowls" terminology, UK-specific rules/formats
- 16-06: Partnership outreach plan for Bowls England + Bowls Scotland

### Phase 17: Australia Market Entry
