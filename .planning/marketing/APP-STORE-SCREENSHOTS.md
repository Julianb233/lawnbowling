# App Store Screenshot Text Overlays

Six screenshots for Apple App Store and Google Play Store listings.
Each entry specifies the headline, subtext, and which app screen to capture.

---

## Screenshot 1: Availability Board

**Headline:** Your bowls, at a glance
**Subtext:** See who's available, who's playing, and what rinks are free — all in real time.
**Screen to capture:** Club availability board showing player status list with green/amber/red indicators and rink allocation grid.
**Background accent colour:** #1B5E20 (forest green)

---

## Screenshot 2: Kiosk Check-In

**Headline:** One-tap check-in at the clubhouse
**Subtext:** No sign-in sheets. No queues. Just tap your name and you're in the draw.
**Screen to capture:** Kiosk mode check-in screen showing the member list with large tap targets and a "Quick Add Guest" button.
**Background accent colour:** #0D47A1 (deep blue)

---

## Screenshot 3: Draw Sheet

**Headline:** See your rink assignment instantly
**Subtext:** The draw goes straight to every player's phone — no more crowding around the noticeboard.
**Screen to capture:** Draw sheet view showing rink assignments with player names, positions (skip, third, second, lead), and rink numbers.
**Background accent colour:** #E65100 (burnt orange)

---

## Screenshot 4: Live Scoring

**Headline:** Score end-by-end, just like the pencil card
**Subtext:** Familiar layout, zero illegible handwriting. Scores update live for everyone.
**Screen to capture:** Scorecard screen showing end-by-end scoring grid with running totals, mimicking a traditional pencil card layout.
**Background accent colour:** #1B5E20 (forest green)

## Screenshot 5: Club Directory

**Headline:** Find a club near you
**Subtext:** Browse clubs on the map, see what's on this week, and join the community.
**Screen to capture:** Club directory map view with club pins, distance indicators, and a club card showing upcoming sessions.
**Background accent colour:** #4A148C (deep purple)

## Screenshot 6: TV Display

**Headline:** Live scores on the clubhouse TV
**Subtext:** Cast the scoreboard to any screen — members and spectators stay in the loop.
**Screen to capture:** TV/large-screen display mode showing multiple rink scores simultaneously with large, readable typography.
**Background accent colour:** #263238 (charcoal)

---

## Design Notes

- **Device frame:** iPhone 15 Pro (6.7") for iOS, Pixel 8 Pro for Android
- **Font:** Playfair Display for headlines, Inter for subtext
- **Layout:** Headline at top (white text on coloured banner), device screenshot centred below
- **Dimensions:** 1290 x 2796px (iOS), 1080 x 2400px (Android)
- **All text must pass WCAG AA contrast on chosen background colours**
