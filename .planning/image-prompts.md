# Lawnbowling App — Image Generation Reference

**Purpose:** Master prompt reference for generating photography-style images via the Gemini Image API using the nano-banana-ui skill. All prompts are engineered for consistent, high-quality, authentic results. Any agent can pick up this file and generate images immediately.

**Do not use:** AI-looking, overly polished, or stock photo aesthetics. Every image should feel like a moment a real person photographed.

---

## Technical Defaults (apply to ALL prompts)

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Camera body | Sony A7IV (full-frame sensor) |
| Available primes | 30mm f/1.2 · 50mm f/1.4 · 75mm f/1.2 |
| Depth of field | Shallow — subject sharp, background creamy bokeh |
| Color grade | Film-like, warm tones, slight desaturation, lifted shadows |
| Light | Natural only — golden hour (first/last hour of sun) or soft overcast |
| Style | Candid documentary — no posing, no eye contact with camera |
| Avoid | Studio lighting, white seamless background, Getty/iStock composition, AI smoothed skin, perfect teeth smiles |

## Category 1 — People Enjoying Lawn Bowling

*Target: diverse ages 25–85, mixed gender, mixed ethnicity. Community feel. The sport as a social ritual.*

---

### 1-A: The Delivery — Hands and Bowl

**Prompt:**
```
Candid documentary photograph, Sony A7IV, 75mm f/1.2, shallow depth of field. Close-up of an elderly woman's hands, age 70s, skin weathered and warm, releasing a dark polished lawn bowl at the moment of delivery. Her grip is precise — thumb below, fingers wrapped. The bowl has just left her fingers, hovering an inch above the closely trimmed emerald green. The jack (small white ball) is visible soft and out-of-focus in the background, roughly 20 metres away. Natural overcast afternoon light, no shadows. Film-like color grade: warm shadows, slight Kodak Portra 400 tonal curve, lifted blacks. Horizontal framing. No eye contact with camera. No logos.
```

## Negative Constraint Reminder (include in every generation call)

Append this to every prompt when calling the Gemini Image API:

```
Do not generate: studio lighting, seamless backgrounds, Getty Images composition, AI-smoothed skin, unnaturally white teeth, stock photo hand poses, corporate diversity poster aesthetics, or any element that reads as staged or artificial.
```

## App Color Context

When images are used as backgrounds or hero images, they will be overlaid with the app's dark green palette:
- Primary dark: `#0A2E12`
- Primary: `#1B5E20`
- Accent text: `#A8D5BA`

Favor images with natural darker edges or areas that can support white text overlaid. Images used purely as card backgrounds may be cropped aggressively — frame subjects with this in mind.
