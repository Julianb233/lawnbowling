# Competitor Analysis: Lawn Bowls Management Software

**Date**: 2026-03-10
**Purpose**: Understand existing lawn bowls software landscape and identify gaps/opportunities for Pick-a-Partner

---

## Key Competitors

### 1. Bowlr (bowlr.co.uk)
**Market position**: 185+ UK clubs, approved by Bowls England & EIBA

- Cloud-based club management system
- Competition management with round robin, knockout stages
- Member score input (self-service)
- League management
- Saved clubs a full working day per week (Bedford Borough case study)
- Built bespoke Bowls England competitions portal

### 2. BowlsNet
**Market position**: Browser-based, no installation needed

- Competition management with multiple divisions/formats
- Player registration and member databases
- Integrated communication systems
- Real-time match results service
- Reporting and analytics (participation trends, club stats)
- Crown green bowling specialized support

### 3. PAMI (pamibowls.com)
**Market position**: Desktop tournament management

- Quick tournament result calculations
- Local club tournament focus

### 4. Playpass
**Market position**: Generic sports management platform

- Lawn bowls schedule creation
- Online score tracking with standings
- League management tools

### 5. Lawn Bowling Club Manager (lawnbowlingclubmanager.com)
**Market position**: Club website + management

- Tournament registrations
- Online league signups
- Email communication tools

### 6. Bowlr App (bowlr.app)
**Market position**: Personal score tracking mobile app

- Frame-by-frame score entry
- Photo scanning for scores
- League/tournament tracking per player
- Multi-platform (iOS + Android), 15 languages

---

## Gap Analysis -- What We're Missing

| Gap | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Results/scoring** | No live scoring or match result recording |
| **Standings/leaderboard** | No tournament standings after matches |
| **Multi-round tournaments** | Only single-draw, no round-robin or multi-stage |
| **Player history** | No match history or win/loss tracking for bowls |
| **Communication** | No notifications for rink assignments or match times |
| **Handicap system** | No support for bowls handicap scoring (0-8 range) |
