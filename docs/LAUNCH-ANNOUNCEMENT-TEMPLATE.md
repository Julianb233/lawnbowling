I'm reaching out because [CLUB_NAME] is already listed in our national lawn bowling directory at **lawnbowl.app** — and I wanted to make sure you know about it.

**LawnBowl** is a free platform built specifically for lawn bowling clubs. It replaces the paper draw sheet with a simple digital system that runs on an iPad at the clubhouse and on players' phones.

### What it does for your club:

- **Tournament Draw Generator** — Singles, Pairs, Triples, Fours. One tap to generate a balanced draw with rink assignments.
- **Live Scoring** — Enter scores end by end on the iPad. Results calculate automatically.
- **Check-In Kiosk** — Set up an iPad at the entrance. Players check in, select their position, and see their rink assignment.
- **Club Directory Listing** — Your club page at lawnbowl.app/clubs/[STATE]/[SLUG] is already live with your address, contact info, and map.
- **TV Scoreboard** — Display live scores on the clubhouse TV.
- **Player Matching** — Help new members find playing partners.

### What it costs:

**Free for players. Free for clubs to get started.** Premium features for larger clubs are available but not required.

### How to get started:

1. Visit **https://www.lawnbowl.app/clubs/claim** to claim your club listing
2. Set up a free account — takes 2 minutes
3. Run your next tournament day using LawnBowl
