# Lawn Bowling Software — Competitive Landscape

## Key Finding

The user's claim that "there is no system in the whole industry worldwide" is **substantially correct**. While pockets of software exist, nothing offers a modern, iPad-first, globally available tournament management + club platform.

---

## Existing Solutions

### Governing Body Platforms (Country-Locked)

| Platform | Country | What it Does | Limitations |
|----------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| **BowlsLink** | Australia | Membership database, competition management, scoring app | AU-only, not exportable, dated UI |
| **BowlsHub** | New Zealand | Centralised membership & competition | NZ-only |
| **Bowls England** | UK | Endorses Bowlr, no proprietary platform | No own tech |
| **Bowls Canada/SA** | Canada, South Africa | No dedicated platforms | Significant gaps |

### Commercial Club Management

| Product | Price | Market | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|---------|-------|--------|-----------|------------|
| **Bowlr** | GBP 8/mo+ (outdoor) GBP 3,000+1,000/yr (indoor) | UK, 185+ clubs | Most feature-complete UK solution, 11 modules | UK-centric, not mobile-first, no draw engine |
| **BowlsManager** | GBP 415/yr + setup | UK | Affordable, simple | Smaller feature set, one-person operation |
| **PAMI** | Dongle license | Australia | Best tournament result calculator | Windows-only, USB dongle, no cloud/mobile |
| **Lawn Bowling Manager** | Undisclosed | North America | Club website + management | Dated, no draw engine, no mobile-first |
| **Global Lawn Bowls** | Tiered subscription | Global (new) | Most ambitious vision, real-time scoring | Very new, maturity unclear |

### Generic Sports Platforms

- **PlayPass** — Free tier, supports 60+ sports, but no bowls-specific features
- **TeamSnap/LeagueApps/ClubSpark** — Zero bowls support

---

## What's Missing (The Opportunity)

1. **No single global platform** — Every solution is country-specific
2. **No iPad-first tournament management** — PAMI requires Windows + USB dongle
3. **No real-time draw generation** with position-based team formation
4. **No live spectator experience** with end-by-end scoring
5. **No cross-format flexibility** (singles/pairs/triples/fours in one tool)
6. **No modern UX** — Most tools look like 2010-2015
7. **No offline capability** — Club greens often have poor WiFi

## Market Size

- ~5,000-8,000 clubs worldwide (AU: 2,000+, UK: 3,500+, plus NZ, Canada, SA, 50+ nations)
- Global lawn bowling market: ~USD 150M (2025), 4% CAGR
- At $400/yr average: $2.2M+ TAM from AU + UK alone
