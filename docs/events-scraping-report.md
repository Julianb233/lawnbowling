## Executive Summary

This report documents the findings from a systematic web research effort to identify lawn bowling events occurring in 2026 across the United States and globally. A total of **46 events** were captured in `scraped-events-2026.json`, spanning 9 states/regions of the US and 3 international jurisdictions (Australia, England, and global/World Bowls).

Several other divisions had data that was either behind dynamic JavaScript rendering (Wix-based sites) or not yet published for 2026.
